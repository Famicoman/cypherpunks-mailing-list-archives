Here is an excellent opportunity for Pauline Hanson supporters in New South
Wales to come meet and hear Australia's best known politician.

Pauline Hanson will be speaking on the failures of successive governments,
the dangers facing Australians and the urgent need for our country to change
direction and once again prioritise the needs of our people.

Tickets will be sold at the door at most venues. Enquiries should be
directed to One Nation Manly office - (02) 9976 0283
