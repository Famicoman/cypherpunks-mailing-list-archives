3. HOW TO LISTEN

To listen and view the keynotes, you will need the following
minimum requirements:

For Windows: Windows 3.1, 95 or NT; 486 machine; a 16-bit sound
card with driver; an Internet connection; 28.8 Kbps modem or
above; 8 MB RAM; 2 MB hard disk space.

For Macintosh: PowerPC (recommended); 68040K processor; 8 MB
RAM; 2 MB hard disk space; an Internet connection; and 28.8 Kbps
modem or above.

You will also need Real Network's Real Media player or
Microsoft's Netshow. The software is downloadable in a couple of
minutes (if you are using Internet Explorer 4.0 you may already
have Netshow). Once the plug-in is installed, it works
seamlessly with your Web browser.

Download the Real Media Player at
http://www.real.com/products/player/downloadrealplayer.html?wp=d
l031198page&src=download

Download Netshow at
http://www.microsoft.com/netshow/download/player.htm

If you have any trouble viewing the keynotes, please see these
Frequently Asked Questions at http://www.audionet.com/faq or
call GartnerGroup's client support center at 203-316-3600.
