plane crash supposedly offers in Volume 2 one of the best 
unclassified recent accounts of High Intensity Radio Frequency 
(HIRF) and electromagnetic interference (EMI).

from the Air Force -- a few copies were publically released
in June 1996 at a Pentagon press conference. We'd like to 
get a copy of Volume 2 to scan the HIRF and EMI parts but 
cannot locate the proper office for a request -- www.af.mil 
does not appear to offer the report.

We'd appreciate hearing from anyone who might have 
access to Volume 2 and would be willing to loan or know 
how we can get it.
