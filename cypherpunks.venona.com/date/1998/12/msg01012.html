| SUBJECT:  Successes and Further Work on Electronic Commerce

|     The Internet and electronic commerce have the potential to
| transform the world economy.  The United States Government is committed
| to a market-driven policy architecture that will allow the new digital 
| economy to flourish while at the same time protecting citizens' rights 
| and freedoms.
