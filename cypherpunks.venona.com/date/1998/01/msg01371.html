>      fingerprints taken."
>      
>      The fingerprint journal was merely a hands-on experiment designed to
>      make the science portion of the test more interesting, Peter Bunton
>      of the MEAP office, said Wednesday.
>

>      can't be obtained without parents' permission. Bunton said he was
