Update of yesterday's post:

The Bureau of Export Administration published 15 January 1998 in 
the Federal Register, "Implementation of the Wassenaar Arrangement 
List of Dual-Use Items: Revisions to the Commerce Control List and 
Reporting Under the Wassenaar Arrangement; Rule."

The rule in TXT format is available in three parts as published:

  http://jya.com/bxa-wa-rule.txt  (354K)
  http://jya.com/bxa-wa-rule2.txt  (373K)
  http://jya.com/bxa-wa-rule3.txt  (32K) 

Or all three parts (742K) in a compressed self-extracting Zip file:

  http://jya.com/bxa-wa-rule.exe (194K)
