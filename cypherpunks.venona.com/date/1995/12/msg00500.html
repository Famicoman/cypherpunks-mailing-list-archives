> Another way is to use a technique called "blinding", in which a random

>so instead of doing the usual RSA decryption:
>
>  m = c^d mod n
>
>we perform:
>
> m = r^-1*(c*r^e)^d mod n
>
>where r is a random number, and  is its inverse.
