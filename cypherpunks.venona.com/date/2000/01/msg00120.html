I created an online poll in the members area of Lycos, Inc.'s Vote 
2000 web site.

This poll is to determine if the placement on the welcome page of 
Campaign 2000 of the party mascots of the Democratic Donkey and the 
Republican Elephant constitute subliminal advertising.

My question is worded:

"Should the political party mascots of parties other than just 
Republicans and Democrats be placed on Lycos's Campaign 2000 welcome 
page?"

You may vote yes or no.

Please go to http://vote2000.lycos.com and sign up and let your voice be heard.
