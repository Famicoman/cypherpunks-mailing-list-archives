Just for some background.

Last year I noticed on Jodi Hoffman's web site 
http://www.gocin.com/ramp that she mentioned that her husband had 
given up on his law practice to work full time on a R.A.M.P. lawsuit 
followed by a request for donations.

Since I knew that Jodi's husband did not quit his practice based on 
public statements by Jodi I thought such an advertisement was 
dishonest.  Jodi defended her web statements saying among other 
things that the donation request didn't generate much money. It 
wasn't until attorney Jim Tyre pointed out that at least under 
Californian law such a statement would constitute fraud and he 
opinioned that he thought Florida law to be similar.  Jodi consulted 
with her husband and then removed the false claim from her web site.
