Welcome to the Cypherpunks Archives hosted at VENONA Secure Solutions.

This is an archive of the glory days of the Cypherpunks mailing list, roughly 1993-2000.

The existing directory tree under "http://cypherpunks.venona.com/date/" will remain as-is; future indexing will be on a new URL linked from this site. Individual messages can be retreived by message-ID, or by month/week, or by content or header search.
