Well, the Y2K crisis seems to have wimped out.
There'll still be random stupid problems for a while,
and Monday's first business day of the year will uncover
a few more failures, but there haven't been massive power failures,
civil unrest, martial law takeovers by the black helicopters, etc.

Some localized shortages of hangover medicine have been reported,
and here in New Orleans we're predicting short supplies for a while,
except for the ever-popular Hair Of The Dog :-)
