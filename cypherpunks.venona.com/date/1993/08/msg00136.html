of my recent roomates!" item.   We're going to do this with Community
Dialtone service.  Toll calling is a separate accounting system than the
local part of the bill, and each person who uses the phone can get an
individual account; the person whose name it's in can require roommates to
get their own toll accounts.  This way no one gets stuck holding the bill.
