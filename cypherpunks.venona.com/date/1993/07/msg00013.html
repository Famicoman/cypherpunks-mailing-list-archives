I've implemented error reporting on remail@extropia.

The errors are forwarded to errors@extropia.wimsey.com.  This is an anon
pool (=mailing list).  Errors are concise, explaining what went wrong.

To subscribe to the error list, send 'subscribe' on the subject line
to errors-request@extropia.wimsey.com.  For help, send 'help'.
