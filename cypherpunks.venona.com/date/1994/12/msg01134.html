> For the sake of a friend, I'm trying to explain the basics of IPSP.
> And I've choked!!!  Could someone please explain it.  What is it,
> how does it work.  ETC.

Simplified, the idea is that you put a cryptographic encapsulation
around your packets and then send them inside other IP packets; the
receiver unwraps them and unencrypts them.
