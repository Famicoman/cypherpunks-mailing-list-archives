ANNOTATED REFERENCES

(All these documents are available on-line. Access information follows.)

=================<a href="ftp://ftp.eff.org/pub/CAF/law/libel_2.IW">
law/libel_2.IW
=================</a>
* Expression -- Libel -- Public Figures and the Net

"Libel, Public Figures, and the Net" by Mike Godwin, an article for
Internet World. Argues that in most cases, replying to defamation on
the Net is more effective than and preferable to a lawsuit.
