receiving mail until the second week in january. At that time Eric

myself reading the list from two accounts. It had been so long since I

shouldn't send unsubscribe messages to it, since only mailing lists
are for subscribing/unsubscribing.

The next day, Eric sent me mail with the right address for unsubscribe
requests.
