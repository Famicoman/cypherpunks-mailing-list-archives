Understand it?  Yes.  Believe it completely, or even believe that it applies 
here?  No. 
 
May's beliefs that pseudo-spoofing can lead to monetary and governmental

razor can even be applied.  
  
``in other words, `entertain me some more, detweiler. I don't believe you

for now, I simply don't give a damn either way, and most of all I am

Convince me.  I'm a fairly hard-core skeptic -- I don't believe *anything*

Posting the evidence would help convince other skeptics, especially if it's
