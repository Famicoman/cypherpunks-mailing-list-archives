I have implemented the "Bcc:" header in my remailer.  It can be used
instead (or in addition) to the "Request-remailing-to:" header.  Addresses
on the "Bcc:" line will not be added to the outgoing mail headers.

The remailer is remail@extropia.wimsey.com.
